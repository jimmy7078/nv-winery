Why do you add USD 25 per bottle handling fee?

Conventional wine retailers have a substantial margin built into the price of the bottles that they sell. Our model is entirely different, so we add USD 25 per bottle to cover the charges for Singapore alcohol excise duty, Singapore GST (sales tax), payment gateway processing fees and end-to-end logistics. Our members like the transparency of the price of the wine displayed separately from taxes and logistics.
